Guest Apartment in Sandanski

Radost Sandanski Apartment overlooks the garden and offers accommodation with a patio and an electric kettle. It is located about 2.7 km from the Episcopal Basilica in Sandanski. There is a casino, a terrace, mountain views and free WiFi throughout. The apartment has a bedroom, a flat-screen satellite TV, an equipped kitchen with a refrigerator and an oven, a washing machine and a bathroom with a shower. There is a garden available to the guests in the apartment, and in the surrounding area there are conditions for hiking and skiing. Spartak Monument is 3.9 km from Apartment Radost Sandanski, while Sandanski Bus Station is 2.4 km away.

Location

Guest apartment Radost is located in the resort town of Sandanski, in a quiet place in the park area. In the immediate vicinity are Park Hotel Pirin and Hotel Perun. Within walking distance is the incredible park of Sandanski. The geographical location of Sandanski is a prerequisite for its extremely favorable climatic features. The air in the city has the lowest content of allergens in all of Bulgaria and is suitable for the treatment of asthmatic, allergic and other diseases. Sandanski is world famous as a natural health resort and spa resort.

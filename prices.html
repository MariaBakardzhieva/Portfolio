Prices

The apartment can accommodate 4 people. We also have a sofa bed, which is intended for children under 10 years old. Their use by an adult costs extra 15 BGN per person.

When renting the apartment, a safety deposit is paid, which will be returned to you when you are ready to leave. The aim of the safety deposit is to compensate damages within the house and on the building itself. All damages will be deducted from the amount of the safety deposit. If damages exceed the amount of the deposit, the customer shall be obliged to restore them to their full extent. The stay of pets is undesirable except after explicit agreement with the owners. The prices for the holidays could be different from the normal rates.

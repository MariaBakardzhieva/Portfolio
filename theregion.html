The Region

Guest Apartment Sandanski is located in the resort town of Sandanski, in a quiet place in the park area. Within walking distance is the incredible park of Sandanski. The geographical location of Sandanski is a prerequisite for its extremely favorable climatic features. The air in the city has the lowest content of allergens in all of Bulgaria and is suitable for the treatment of asthmatic, allergic and other diseases. Sandanski is world famous as a natural health resort and spa resort.

The air in Sandanski has the lowest content of allergens in Bulgaria and the highest natural healing properties. The city is world famous as a spa resort. In the municipality of Sandanski there are over 80 springs with a temperature of 42 - 81 ° C, and in the area of the town there are about 20. The combination of favorable natural resources, together with the richness of mineral springs with different thermality, low mineralization and rich chemical composition, cures many diseases and allows for outdoor bathing throughout the year. The park has two outdoor pools.
